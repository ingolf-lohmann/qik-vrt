# Privacy, Proportionality, and Emergency Boundaries

Traceability is bounded by dignity, privacy, and proportionality.

## Privacy rule
Collect the minimum necessary information for the stated purpose.

## Proportionality rule
Prefer reversible, least-intrusive measures.

## Protected review rule
Where public disclosure would harm people or enable misuse, review may be performed by protected responsible nodes.

## Emergency rule
Concrete plausible danger can justify protective action before full proof. Emergency action must be documented, reviewable, proportionate, and not treated as punishment.

## Anti-surveillance rule
This framework must not be used as a general surveillance system.
