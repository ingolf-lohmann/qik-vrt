## Scientific boundary

Formal-environment completion does not convert empirical, interpretive, background, metaphysical, spiritual, retrocausal, quantum-gravitational or normative content into mathematical proof. The release preserves those epistemic distinctions and never treats conditional theorems as unconditional statements about nature.
